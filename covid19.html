COVID '19

Safety

For everyone's safety, we have delayed the previous year's event. With the ongoing vaccination programs, our hope is that there will be no need for strict social distancing measures in September '21.

Measures

However, if needed, we will take appropriate measures. For example, we might stagger the festivities in smaller groups. More details will be available as the event approaches.
