COVID '19

Safety

In this moment, there are no restrictions in place for events like ours, with less than 150 persons indoors.

Measures

However, for everyone's safety, we recommend the now classical precautions, where every participant should have a document attesting one of the following: a) full vaccination completed at least 10 days prior to the event; b) a negative COVID PCR test taken at most 72 hours prior to the event, or a negative antigen test taken at most 24 hours prior to the event; c) a medical certificate attesting recovery from a COVID illness in the past 180 days.
