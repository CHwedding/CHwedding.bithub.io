FAQ, Tips

QUESTION

Is there parking for the ceremony or reception?

ANSWER

Limited parking is available both at the church, for the ceremony, as well as at the Cantacuzino Castle (about 20 cars).

QUESTION

I am vegetarian / vegan / have a food allergy, can I make a special request?

ANSWER

Please let us know before the 1st of June so we can take this into account when planning the menu.

QUESTION

Are kids welcome?

ANSWER

Please let us know if you intend bringing your children and we will organize a children's corner.

QUESTION

Will shops be open on Sunday?

ANSWER

Almost all shops are open every day (including Sunday) and accept card payments. The Romanian currency is the Romanian LEU (RON).

QUESTION

Is there a Taxi app?

ANSWER

Yes: CleverTaxi. Uber is also available.
